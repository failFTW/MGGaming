The Ranks:


      Promotion Ladder: Guest --> Member --> Survivor --> Veteran --> Trusted --> VIP --> Donator

    Guest

     The default rank.
     To Get Promoted: Ask a Staff Member.
    Member
     Members are what make up the majority of the server.
      To Get Promoted: Be loyal, you have to be on the server for long enough for a high-ranking Staff member to trust you (About a week)
    Survivor

     When you earn this rank, you get access to a few more things than Members do (will be listed later).
     To Get Promoted: Either donate or help with a server-wide project.
    Veteran

     Having this rank means you have been on the server for a long time, like a year.
     To Get Promoted: Donate, apply for Mod, or just be super awesome.
    Trusted

     Having this rank means I trust you. A lot.
     To Get Promoted: Donate, apply for Mod, or just be super awesome.
    VIP
     The highest non-donaor rank you can get. (More information to come)
      To Get Promoted: Donate.
   Donator Ranks:
    You can only get this when you donate.
    Donator Promotion Ladder: Stone Donator --> Iron Donator --> Gold Donator --> Lapis Donator --> Diamond Donator
   Click here for more info about Donator ranks.
   Moderator
    They have almost magical powers. Powers include: /kick, /time, /say, /tp, /mute, and /back.
