   The Ranks:


      Promotion Ladder: Guest --> Member --> Trusted --> VIP --> Donator

    Guest

     The default rank.
     To Get Promoted: Ask a Staff Member.
    Member
     Members are what make up the majority of the server.
      To Get Promoted: Be loyal, you have to be on the server for long enough for a high-ranking Staff member to trust you.
    Trusted

     When you earn this rank, you get access to a few more things than Members do (will be listed later).
     To Get Promoted: Either donate or help with a server-wide project.
    VIP
     The highest non-donaor rank you can get. (More information to come)
      To Get Promoted: Donate.
